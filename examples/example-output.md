# Example Output: Positioning Alignment Review

## Quick verdict

**Alignment score:** 58/100  
**Review status:** Needs major revision  
**Most important fix:** Reframe the hero around compliant payout activation for platforms, not generic global money movement.

The draft is clean and short, but it over-broadens the product and misses the brief’s sharpest positioning. It includes developer-friendly APIs, dashboards, and payout tracking, but it underplays recipient onboarding, compliance controls, platform-specific use cases, and proof points. The current hero also uses two phrases the brief specifically says to avoid.
