## Messaging and positioning brief

Product: AtlasPay Payout Links

Audience:
- Operations and finance leaders at marketplaces, creator platforms, and gig economy platforms.
- Secondary audience: developers who need to integrate payouts without building recipient onboarding from scratch.

Core positioning:
- AtlasPay Payout Links is the fastest way for platforms to launch compliant global payouts without building an internal payout portal.
- The product is not a general payments platform. It is specifically for recipient onboarding and payout activation.

Value pillars:
1. Launch compliant payout flows quickly without building custom recipient onboarding.
2. Reduce operations workload by letting recipients self-serve tax forms, bank details, identity checks, and payout status.
3. Give teams built-in compliance controls, including KYC/KYB checks, sanctions screening, and an audit trail.
4. Support developer flexibility through APIs, hosted links, and payout status webhooks.

Differentiators:
- Combines hosted recipient onboarding, payout activation, and compliance workflows in one product.
- Designed for platforms with many recipients, not one-off business payments.

Proof points:
- Supports recipient onboarding in 80+ countries.
- SOC 2 Type II controls are available for enterprise customers.
- Typical recipient onboarding takes less than 5 minutes when the recipient has required documents ready.

Approved language:
- Use “recipient onboarding,” “payout activation,” and “compliant global payouts.”
- Prefer “platforms” over “businesses” when describing the primary audience.

Avoid:
- Do not describe the product as “for any business.”
- Do not say “move money anywhere.”
- Do not position it as a full payments platform.
